Our Restaurant

The Perfect Place For An Unforgettable Italian Experience

Rooted in Italian heritage, Olivae brings warmth and authenticity to every table. Our nature-inspired space invites you to slow down, connect, and truly savour the moment.

Every corner of Olivae is thoughtfully designed to reflect the beauty of Italy — from hand-selected seasonal ingredients to elegant, artisanal plating. We craft each dish with heritage, passion, and a deep respect for culinary tradition, making every visit a genuinely memorable occasion worth sharing.
